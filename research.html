Research Paper • 2026

How Do Stock Markets React to Natural Disasters?

A comparative analysis of the 2011 Great East Japan Earthquake, Hurricane Katrina (2005), and the 2023 Türkiye Earthquakes.

Research Question

How do stock market reactions differ across countries following major natural disasters, and what role do economic resilience, institutional strength and sectoral exposure play?

Key Findings

  • Human casualties are a poor predictor of market decline.
  • Economic resilience explains recovery better than disaster size.
  • Sectoral exposure strongly influences market performance.
  • Government and central bank responses affect investor confidence.
  • Trading halts can significantly alter measured recovery.
