Featured Project

How Do Stock Markets React to Natural Disasters?

A comparative analysis of the 2011 Great East Japan Earthquake, Hurricane Katrina (2005), and the 2023 Türkiye Earthquakes.

This paper examines how stock markets respond to natural disasters and evaluates the roles of economic resilience, institutional strength and sectoral exposure.
